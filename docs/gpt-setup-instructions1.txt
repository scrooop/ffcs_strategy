Forward Factor Formula

FF = (Front_IV - Fwd_IV) / Fwd_IV

Where:
- Front_IV = ATM implied volatility for front expiration
- Fwd_IV = Forward implied volatility between front and back expirations
- FF > 0 means front IV is "hot" (backwardation) → calendar spread opportunity


Trade Thresholds (from video transcript)

General Rule: FF ≥ 0.20 is tradable

Optimized Thresholds (~20 trades/month):
- 30-60 DTE: FF ≥ 0.23
- 30-90 DTE: FF ≥ 0.23
- 60-90 DTE: FF ≥ 0.20

Returns turn positive: Around FF ≥ 0.10 to 0.20
